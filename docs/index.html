The Dance Physio
Physiotherapy for Dancers

Ever wanted to know how to become the most fit, strong, flexible, injury-free dancer you can be so that you can dance at your peak?

The Dance Physio can help!

My passion lies in treating dancers. So any dancers out there who would like treatment from a professional dancer and physiotherapist that understands the dancer's body, you’ve come to the right place. My dream is to help as many dancers as I can to become the best versions of themselves so they can reach their dance and fitness goals.

Services available:

  • Online and in person consultations
  • Dance physio educational workshops
  • Dance lessons (individual/group). Multiple styles available

Who am I?

Hi! My name is Jessica Lai. I’m a practicing physiotherapist during the day and a professional dancer in the evenings.

I graduated from the University of Sydney in 2009 and can treat any musculoskeletal issues but my passion lies in treating dancers so I have sought specialised education in this field.

I’m currently teaching Salsa, Cha cha, Brazillian Zouk, Merengue, Bachata, Ladies styling, Spins training, Mambo, wedding dances, private individual & group dance lessons in one of the biggest Latin dance schools in Australia. My dance journey started when I was 7 years old in latin & ballroom and I’ve dabbled in many different dance styles since then.

I’ve won multiple national titles and have performed, judged dance competitions, taught dance & physio related workshops all over Sydney, interstate and around the world.
